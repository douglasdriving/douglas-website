Ambition is my enemy

2024-11-28 (added)

I dream about the future of me and what I might become if I try hard enough: a respected thinker, a genius creator, and a successful entrepreneur. Wherever and whomever I am, I can think about a future in which I am “better.” The ideal self always shifts forward.

This results in a constant gap between me and my ideal self, a distance that keeps telling me what I am now is insufficient. The dream is always bigger than reality, and reality always seems to lack something.

Never reaching the point of being enough drains me and breaks me down. I never get to pluck the fruit from the tree that I am growing and experience the joy of being who I am.

The solution cannot be to work harder, but to let go; of dreams, of the future, of ambition, and control. To focus on being instead of on becoming.
